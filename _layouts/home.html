Introduction

Welcome to the Hackathon Planning Kit. This planning kit provides a detailed description of 12 aspects for hackathon design and tradeoffs between design decisions which were deduced from the empirical studies of 10 hackathons held by scientific communities, a corporation, and universities as well as published literature.

This planning kit will support anyone who wish to organize hackathons for the first time or have organized hackathons before in designing a hackathon to achieve the goals that they want.
