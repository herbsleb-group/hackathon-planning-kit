The most common goals that today's hackathon organizers aim to achieve are community building, learning, and entrepreneurship. The organizers and participants may also have other goals in mind which may be orthogonal to the event's primary goals. We therefore urge organizers to be aware of potential design trade-offs and that there is no one-size-fit-all solution to hackathon design.

We provide an optimal hackathon design for community building, and learning, and entrepreneurship.
