The two most common goals that today's hackathon organizers aim to achieve are community building and entrepreneurship. The organizers and participants may also have other goals in mind which may be orthogonal to the primary goals stated above. However, there is no one-size-fit-all solution to hackathon design and that the organizers need to be aware of design trade-offs.

We recommend optimal hackathon design for community building and entrepreneurship.
