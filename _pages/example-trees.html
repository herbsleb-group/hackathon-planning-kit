The two most common goals that today's hackathon organizers aim to achieve are community building, and entrepreneurship. However, organizers and participants may have other goals in mind which may be orthogonal their primary goals. For example, hackathon for building a great technical artifact might not be possible if people want to learn at the same time.

Hackathon organizers therefore need to understand that there is no one-size-fit-all solution to hackathon design, and we recommend optimal hackathon features for community building, and entrepreneurship.
