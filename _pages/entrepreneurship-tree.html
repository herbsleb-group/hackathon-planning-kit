The entrepreneurship hackathons are typically held for entrepreneurship or creating start-ups. One important aspect of this type of hackathon is follow-up activities that could increase the sustainability of projects. Specifically, organizers should connect the hackathon teams with potential stakeholders and create opportunities to present and showcase the hackathon projects to a larger audience beyond the event participants.

In the tree above,
  • the root node represents the goal,
  • all interior nodes are decision nodes and contain high-level features (e.g., projects, teams, process, mentor, and competition)
  • the leaf nodes contain low-level features, and
  • the activated links colored in red show the paths to the important features that the organizers need to focus on but the inactivated links should not be discarded.
